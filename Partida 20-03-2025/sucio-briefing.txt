Description
Recon units have spotted many enemy forces getting ready for an attack. These units would be the primary threat to nearby allies should The enemy attack them. To prevent this, Command has demanded the elimination of these units. recon units have spotted many enemy forces massing deep inside enemy territory. These units could pose a major risk to our ground forces when the ground war begins. To prevent this, Command has ordered the destruction of these units.




Tasks
Destroy all ballistic missiles designated objective MARS (WP1).
Destroy all tanks designated objective CULDROSE (WP2).
Destroy the tracking radars at objective TURF (WP3).

Desarrollo de la misión:
1. El grupo Jury despegará en cabeza y establecera una orbita alrededor del Carrier, a la espera del grupo Sting.
2. El grupo Sting despegará dirección WP3, con la tarea de acabar con el SA-2 enemigo.
3. Grupo Jury iniciará escolta de grupo Sting.
4. Grupos strike, Boar y Springfield estarán listos para despegar inmediatamente una vez grupo Sting esté atacando WP3.
5. Con la superioridad aérea ganada, Boar y Springfield tienen luz verde para dirigirse a WP2 y WP1 y acabar con todas las unidades allí presentes.
6. Con la superioridad aérea ganada, Sting se une a la escolta de Strike junto a Jury.
